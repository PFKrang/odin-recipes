Japanese Beef Curry

Description

About

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chuck beef
  • 1 onion
  • 3 carrots
  • 3 potatoes
  • 1-2 bricks of mild or medium curry roux

Instructions

  1. Get a large pot, sear the beef and saute the onions
  2. Pour enough water to cover the meat and onions, add the roux and mix
  3. Cover and cook until the meat is tender, on medium heat it can take up to an hour
  4. Add potatoes and carrots, cook til everything is fork tender
  5. Serve over rice
