Corned Beef

Description

About

Ingredients

  • Get pre-brined corned beef
  • 4 large carrots
  • 6 to 8 russet potatoes
  • 1 Six pack of Guiness or stout of choice

Instructions

  1. Peel carrots and potatoes
  2. Cut potatoes and carrots into even size pieces
  3. Drop corn beef into slow cooker with the included pack of seasoning
  4. Add carrots and potatoes to the slow cooker
  5. Pour two of the beers over the meat and veggies
  6. Cook on high for 4-6 hours or low for 7-8 hours
  7. Enjoy with the rest of the beer
