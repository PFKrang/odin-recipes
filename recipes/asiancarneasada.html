Asian Carne Asada

Description

Carne Asada but with an asian twist

About

Ingredients

  • Skirt Steak
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Mirin
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Place meat in a container
  2. Mix together all other ingredients in a bowl
  3. Pour mixture over the meat and marinate for 12-24 hours, flipping once halfway throught the time frame
  4. Grill the meat
